College Admissions Essay Coaching
by Deborah Bolzan

Helping students share their stories.

I take the stress out of the writing portion of the college application process. I advise students how to harness their creativity, convey their uniqueness and express themselves articulately and concisely to reveal the most accurate, authentic and impactful picture of themselves to the college admissions reader.

I coach students through the essay process from beginning to end. This includes brainstorming ideas, discussing the outline and structure of the essay(s), and reviewing each draft, with unlimited feedback. Students write their own essays, in their own voice, and provide the university another dimension of themselves to consider, beyond the straightforward numbers that rule the rest of the application.

About Deborah

After a long career in speechwriting and executive communications at IBM’s European headquarters in Paris, I moved to the New York City area and pursued a new path in college counseling.

I hold a bachelors degree in English from the University of Virginia, a masters degree in Integrated Marketing Communications from Northwestern University’s Medill School of Journalism, and a certificate with distinction in College Counseling from UCLA Extension. My UCLA certificate included a capstone practicum project as a writing coach at the Lycée Français de New York, where I continue to provide writing support to high school students applying to universities around the world, particularly in the United States and the UK.

I am an alumna interviewer for Northwestern, a mother of three (two who have already successfully completed the college application process), and a dual citizen of the U.S. and France.

I know that the college application process can be fabulously stressful for the student and the family, and I enjoy allaying that stress by working with students on the most subjective and arguably the most compelling portion of the application — the essay — which I believe can truly sway an admissions decision when it is well-written and persuasive.
